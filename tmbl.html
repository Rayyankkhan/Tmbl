TBML Solutions
Leading the Fight Against Trade-Based Money Laundering
At TBML LLC, we harness the power of AI to deliver a comprehensive suite of modular solutions designed to detect and prevent Trade-Based Money Laundering (TBML). Our technology empowers financial institutions to strengthen compliance, mitigate risks, and safeguard their operations.
Price iQ
Price Due Diligence
Price iQ™ is a due diligence tool that ensures fair market value by detecting over and under invoicing through automated pre and post transaction price variance analysis. It verifies prices against reliable sources and supports transparency with underlying sale contracts for compliance in trade transactions.

Price iQ
Bank’s Trade Risk Profiling
Price iQ™ is a due diligence tool that ensures fair market value by detecting over and under invoicing through automated pre and post transaction price variance analysis.
Learn More
DUG iQ
Dual Use Goods Check
DUG iQ™ is an advanced module for checking dual-use goods, designed to swiftly and accurately identify items with both civilian and military applications. It automates the verification process using multiple trusted sources, eliminating the need for manual checks and duplicate entries.
Learn More
Screen iQ
Govt. And Bank Policy Check
Screen iQ™ within TBML verifies HS (Harmonized System) codes against government regulations to ensure compliance. It checks the quantity of goods traded and confirms that HS codes align with product descriptions. Detailed information, including specific HS codes for each item, is provided on forms such as Export Invoice Form and Export Freight Entry.
Learn More
Vessel iQ
Vessel Tracking
Vessel iQ™ offers in-depth historical data on ships, enabling users to search by ship name, MMSI, or IMO number and specify a date range. It provides detailed records of past movements, including location, speed, navigational status, and destination. Vessel iQ™ also includes historical data on vessel traffic in designated zones, ports, or regions and conducts sanctions checks to verify vessel information for selected periods.
Learn More
Container iQ
Container Tracking
Container iQ™ is an advanced module for tracking the movement and status of shipping containers. It provides real-time and historical data on container locations, transit routes, and handling events. Users can monitor container movements across different ports and logistics hubs, assess transit times, and review historical shipping data.
Learn More
Risk iQ
Customers Risk Profiling
Risk iQ™, an essential component of AML solution, is designed to pinpoint and manage potential risks in trade transactions. It features a flexible risk weightage system that provides real-time validation of customer risk profiles, including detailed insights into traded goods, buyers, sellers, and other transaction parties.
Learn More
Transact iQ
Transaction Monitoring System
Transact iQ™ is a sophisticated real-time monitoring tool for detecting money laundering and terrorist financing risks. Powered by AI, it identifies suspicious activities through over 80+ TBML scenarios and continuously updates to address emerging risks.
